Zepee is for founders who need a great demo fast — without hiring an editor or learning video tools. Point it at your site, get a demo that looks great, and run it again anytime your site changes so it stays up to date.

How it works

1. Point it at your site

Give Zepee your website link and tell it what to show. It works out the rest — you don't write anything.

2. It records your site

Zepee clicks, scrolls, and types through your real site like a person would — even pages behind a login.

3. Get your video and screenshots

You get a video and clear screenshots, ready to post on Product Hunt, X, or your website.
